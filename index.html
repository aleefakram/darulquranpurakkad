About Darul Qur'an

Darul Qur'an Purakkad is a center of excellence dedicated to the holistic development of the next generation. We provide a structured, disciplined, and nurturing environment for young students from age 5 up to 15.

Operated under a registered Non-Profit Organization, our mission is to make high-quality spiritual and secular education accessible. We rely on community support to maintain our standards and facilities.

Our unique model ensures students become proficient memorizers of the Holy Qur'an (Hafiz) while simultaneously pursuing high-quality modern academics.
