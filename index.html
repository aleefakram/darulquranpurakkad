About Darul Qur'an

Darul Qur'an Purakkad is more than a school; it is a center of excellence dedicated to the holistic development of the next generation. We provide a structured, disciplined, and nurturing environment for young students from age 5 up to 15.

Our unique model ensures students become proficient memorizers of the Holy Qur'an (Hafiz) while simultaneously pursuing high-quality modern academics, preparing them for both the hereafter and challenges of the contemporary world.

  • Integrated Academic & Spiritual Program
  • Safe, Supervised Residential Campus
  • Character Building and Moral Education
