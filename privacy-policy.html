2. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you voluntarily submit our contact form:

  • Your Name
  • Email Address
  • Mobile Number
  • Message Content

We also store your theme preference (light/dark mode) in your browser's local storage. This is not personally identifiable and is stored entirely on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

The personal data you submit through the contact form is used solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiries about admissions, donations, or general queries
  • Follow up on your request if necessary

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond responding to your inquiry.

4. Donations

Our website provides bank account details and a UPI QR code to facilitate donations. We do not collect, process, or store any payment or financial information through this website. All transactions are carried out directly between the donor and the institution's bank account via the donor's own banking app or service.

All donations are voluntary contributions and are non-refundable. Any transaction-related issues should be resolved through the respective banking institution. For donation-related queries, please contact us directly.

5. How We Store Your Data

Contact form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify, our website hosting provider. Netlify processes form data in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

We do not maintain a separate database of form submissions beyond what Netlify provides.

6. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services that may process limited visitor data:

Google Fonts

We load fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When you visit our site, your browser sends a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Maps

We embed a Google Maps iframe in our Contact section to show our location. Google may set cookies and collect data through this embed. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Netlify

Our site is hosted on Netlify, which may collect server access logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

This website does not set any first-party cookies. However:

  • We use browser local storage to remember your light/dark theme preference. This data never leaves your device.
  • Third-party services (Google Maps, Google Fonts) may set their own cookies as described above.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law or as described in the third-party services section above.

9. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. Old submissions are periodically reviewed and deleted.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent — you may withdraw consent at any time for future processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at darulquranpurakkad123@gmail.com.

11. Children's Privacy

While Darul Quran Purakkad is an educational institution serving young students, the website itself does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Parents or guardians should submit contact form inquiries on behalf of minors.
